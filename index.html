About Us

Welcome to Juliette Vege Recipe. I'm Juliette, a part-time homecook who's inspired by vegetarian/vegan recipes! The main reason why I created this website is to let all of you know that you don't have to be a master chef to get cooking. Why? Because I am here to help you test out all the best recipes you can possibly find. I'll take the failure to let you succeed!

Happy cooking!


Juliette Maignan

My name is Thomas and I'm apprentice web designer. Juliette and I are together for almost 7 years and we both find cooking to be our passion. We often share lots of recipe as well as cooking experience to each other and thought that it'd be fun to share it with you guys too. I hope you also enjoy this cookbook as much as we do!

Cheers,
